About EQI Coin

EQI Coin (EQIM) is a decentralized BEP-20 payment token built on BNB Smart Chain, designed for fast, low-cost, and transparent peer-to-peer transactions. Our mission is to bridge traditional payment systems with decentralized finance.

Our Mission

EQI Coin was created to solve a fundamental problem in the crypto space: the gap between complex DeFi protocols and everyday payment needs. We believe that sending value should be as simple as sending a message — instant, borderless, and virtually free.

Built on BNB Smart Chain for its speed (3-second blocks) and low fees (<$0.01 per transaction), EQIM is designed for real-world utility: merchant payments, peer-to-peer transfers, and cross-border remittances.
