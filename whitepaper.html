1. Abstract

EQI Coin (ticker: EQIM) is a BEP-20 utility token deployed on BNB Smart Chain, purpose-built for fast, low-cost peer-to-peer payments. With a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens, zero transaction tax, and full smart contract transparency, EQIM provides a simple, efficient medium of exchange for everyday transactions and cross-border transfers.

This document outlines the technical architecture, economic design, security model, and long-term vision of the EQI ecosystem.

2. Problem Statement

Traditional payment systems suffer from several critical limitations:

  • High fees: Cross-border wire transfers cost $25-50+ and take 3-5 business days.
  • Limited access: 1.4 billion adults remain unbanked, unable to participate in the global economy.
  • Opacity: Transaction fees, exchange rates, and processing delays are often hidden from end users.
  • Complexity: Many DeFi protocols require technical knowledge, creating barriers to adoption.

Existing crypto payment solutions either have high gas fees (Ethereum), slow confirmations (Bitcoin), or introduce unnecessary complexity through reflection taxes, rebase mechanics, or inflationary models.

3. Solution: EQI Coin

EQI Coin addresses these issues through simplicity and efficiency:

  • Zero tax: No buy/sell tax, no reflection, no hidden fees. 100% of transferred value reaches the recipient.
  • Fixed supply: 1 billion tokens with no mint function. Supply is permanently capped.
  • Low cost: BNB Smart Chain transactions cost <$0.01, making micro-payments viable.
  • Instant finality: 3-second block times provide near-instant transaction confirmation.
  • Universal compatibility: Standard BEP-20 interface works with every BSC wallet and DEX.

4.2 Key Features

  • ERC-1967 Proxy: Transparent upgrade mechanism with on-chain governance timelock.
  • Batch Transfers: Gas-efficient multi-recipient transfers for airdrops and distributions.
  • EIP-2612 Permit: Gasless approvals via signed messages (meta-transactions support).
  • Owner Controls: Standard ownership model with transferability. No pausable or blacklist functions.

6. Security Model

  • Verified Source Code: Full contract source is published and verified on BscScan for public audit.
  • No Hidden Functions: No selfdestruct, no hidden mint, no blacklist capabilities.
  • Proxy Transparency: ERC-1967 implementation address is publicly readable on-chain via the standard storage slot.
  • Timelock Governance: Upgrade requests require a timelock period, preventing instant malicious upgrades.
  • Open Source: Contract code follows OpenZeppelin standards and is available for community review.

The team is committed to pursuing a formal third-party security audit as the project reaches sufficient maturity and treasury allocation. Audit firm selection will be announced publicly.

8. Governance

EQI Coin currently operates under a centralized development model with a transparent team. As the ecosystem matures, the project plans to transition toward community governance:

  • Phase 1 (Current): Team-managed development with public communication.
  • Phase 2 (Q4 2026): Community proposals via snapshot voting for key decisions.
  • Phase 3 (2027): On-chain governance module for contract upgrades and treasury management.
